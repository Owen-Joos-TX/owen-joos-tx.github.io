This site is all about the rock band Stage Fright!

Our Story
We started our band in the summer of 2024, having been brought together through our shared fear of being on stage, only overcome by our desire to share our music.

Our music is inspired by the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Linkin Park, and samples of elevator music.
